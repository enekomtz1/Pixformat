Convert WebP to PNG or JPG. Why and How

WebP is Google's modern image format offering superior compression, but not every application accepts it. If you have downloaded a WebP image and need to share it in a more compatible format, Pixformat makes it easy to convert WebP to PNG or WebP to JPG in seconds.

When to Convert WebP to PNG

Choose PNG when you need lossless quality or transparency support, for example, converting a WebP logo or graphic for use in print or design software that does not support WebP.

When to Convert WebP to JPG

Choose JPG when file size matters more than transparency. Converting WebP photos to JPG produces widely compatible files suitable for email, social media, and documents.

Convert PNG to JPG and JPG to PNG Online

PNG and JPG are the two most widely used image formats. Converting PNG to JPG reduces file size significantly for photographic images, making them faster to upload and share. Converting JPG to PNG is useful when you need lossless quality for editing or when the destination requires PNG format.

With Pixformat, you can convert PNG to JPG or JPG to PNG instantly. Drop your files, select the output format, and download. Batch conversion is supported: convert dozens of images at once and download them as a ZIP archive.

Convert SVG, GIF, and BMP to Modern Formats

Pixformat accepts SVG, GIF, and BMP as input formats and converts them to WebP, PNG, or JPEG.

SVG to PNG

Convert GIF images to PNG for lossless static frames, or to WebP for dramatically smaller file sizes. This is ideal for extracting still frames from animated GIFs.

BMP to JPG

BMP files are uncompressed and very large. Convert BMP to JPG to reduce file size by 90% or more while preserving visual quality.

Convert Images to WebP and AVIF for Faster Websites

WebP and AVIF are modern image formats designed to reduce file sizes without sacrificing visual quality. Converting your PNG or JPG images to WebP can shrink file sizes by 25–35% on average, directly improving page load times and Core Web Vitals scores.

AVIF goes even further. On supported browsers, AVIF delivers 30–50% smaller files than JPEG at equivalent quality. Pixformat detects AVIF support automatically and enables it as an output option when your browser supports it. Use Pixformat's PNG-to-WebP converter or JPG-to-WebP converter to optimize your website images in bulk.
